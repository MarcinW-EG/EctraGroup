INRUNNER ADVANTAGE

Designed for stable mechanics, cleaner airflow and long-term durability.

ECTRA uses an inrunner architecture: the rotating system is compact and mechanically stiff, while the stator is in direct thermal contact with the housing. This is the right direction for serious HVAC fan systems.

01

Higher mechanical stiffness

Lower radial and axial deflection, better alignment of the rotor-bearing system and reduced vibration risk.

02

Cleaner fan aerodynamics

No large rotating motor body in the flow axis. Less disturbance, lower pressure loss and better system efficiency.

03

Lower bearing loads

Lower rotating motor mass reduces bearing stress and supports longer service life in continuous HVAC operation.

04

Better winding cooling

The external stator is directly connected to the housing, improving heat transfer and reducing winding temperature.
